About Us

Our Story

Founded by like minded technologies thathad their fair share of problems in recruiting the best talent to execute on their business problems, Allshore technologies is a technology and talent management company that emphasizes on leaving clients in total control of their staffing & talent strategy by proactively listening to their needs, promptly delivering on the commitments and not being pushy and misleading with excessive marketing and sales.

At Allshore we want to grow consciously, build a brand that is synonymous with exceptional value and uncompromising quality, be intentionally lean and uber responsive to our clients, needs. We spend most of our capital on actual talent rather than wasting it on bloated recruitment teams, unwanted processes and hierarchy, layers and layers of red tape.

Our Engagement

No matter what your needs are, we have a model that suits you:

Managed Services

In this model, Allshore takes full responsibility for managing the client's IT staffing needs. This includes sourcing, recruiting, onboarding, training, and managing the IT professionals. This model provides a turnkey solution for clients that need to manage their IT staffing needs but do not have the resources or expertise to do so.

Project-based/Staffing Augmentation

In this model, clients engage Allshore professionals to work on a specific project or set of projects. This model provides flexibility and allows clients to manage their costs effectively.

Contract Staffing

In this model, clients engage Allshore professionals on a contractual basis for a specific period, which can range from a few months to several years. This model is ideal for companies that need IT professionals for specific projects or short-term assignments.

Contract-to-Hire

In this model, clients engage Allshore to provide resources on a contract basis with the intention of hiring them as full-time employees at the end of the contract period. This model allows clients to evaluate the IT professionals' skills and fit with the company culture before making a long-term commitment.

Direct Placement

In this model, Allshore recruits IT professionals for clients to fill permanent positions. Allshore charges a one-time fee for the service. This model is ideal for clients that need to hire IT professionals for permanent positions but do not have the resources or expertise to recruit them.
